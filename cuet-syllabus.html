Note: CUET consists of three sections: Language, Domain Specific Subjects, and General Test.

Section IA & IB: Languages

Reading Comprehension

Factual, Literary, and Narrative passages

Verbal Ability

Rearranging parts, Choosing correct words

Vocabulary

Synonyms, Antonyms, One-word substitution

Section II: Domain Specific Subjects

Subject Choice

Candidates can choose up to 6 Domain Subjects

Syllabus Reference

Based entirely on Class 12 NCERT Syllabus

Popular Domains

Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Accountancy, Eco, etc.

Section III: General Test

General Knowledge

Current Affairs, General Mental Ability

Numerical Ability

Basic Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Mensuration (Std 8 level)

Reasoning

Logical and Analytical Reasoning
